Trip Description: ( offer avaliable time: July 2013 to Feb. 2014 )

New York has a world of possibilities to offer any traveler, from shopping on 5th Avenue to Long Island, and the exquisite Hamptons. Spend the day sightseeing, spotting all those famous buildings from the movies, shopping, and strolling through Central Park. Visit a broadway show, and see a fantastic show -many with some recognizable cast members, then enjoy the buzz of the city by night in the restaurants and dance til dawn.

Estimation Price: $728 -$1400
Travel Time:7 Days
Departure Cities: Whole Europe
Airline: American Airline (Economic Class)
Hotel: Hillton, Double-bed Standard Room
Meal: Breakfast included
