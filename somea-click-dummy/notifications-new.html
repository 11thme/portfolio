Notification Alert

Enable Alert means:

All Warning messages like " Near to end of countdown date" for App Countdown 1 will show up as a Popup Window in the future

See a sample here

you can also change this setting in "Settings -> Notification Settings"

Disable Alert means:

All Warning messages like " Near to end of countdown date" for App Countdown 1 will NOT show up as a Popup Window in the future

you can also change this setting in "Settings -> Notification Settings"

Notification Mute

Enable Mute means:

All Warning messages like " Near to end of countdown date" for App Countdown 1 will NOT show up anymore in Notification Center in the future

you can change this setting back in "Settings -> Notification Settings" anytime

Disable Mute means:

All Warning messages like " Near to end of countdown date" for App Countdown 1 will Continue show up anymore in Notification Center/E-mail/Popup in the future.

you can change this setting back in "Settings -> Notification Settings" anytime
